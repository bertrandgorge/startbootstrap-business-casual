French Riviera An unexpected parenthesis

Located in the heart of the Nice-Cannes-Grasse "Golden Triangle", in the hinterland, Mas d'OSMEEA is ideally located between sea and mountain, away from the main roads of the region (2.5 Kms from the heart of the village of Roquefort les pins)

Bastide of character

Surrounded by lush greenery, Mas d'OSMEEA dates from 1856 for the most recent part and probably the 1750s for the oldest part. It was probably once a gathering point around the well and bread ovens.

Stéphanie and Olivier bought this bastide at the end of 2013. Since then, they have renovated and worked to improve this environment, to offer you an unexpected parenthesis.

In the field, you will enjoy tree species of the region: Olive trees, mulberry, mulberry, linden.

On one of the terraces, a Provencal style pool and secure will be at your disposal.

Multiple configurations From 1 to 21 people

The Mas of OSMEEA consists of two independent spaces:

A Villa of 280m² with 5 bedrooms (a potential capacity of 9 people). This part is for rent 3 weeks in summer, usually the first 3 weeks of August.

A Guest House, with 4 modular accommodations, for a maximum capacity of 12 people.

All rooms in the guest house are equipped with:

  • WIFI
  • a private bathroom with toilet
  • a courtesy tray
  • a hair dryer
  • towels
  • bed linen
  • a reversible air conditioning

The organization of the building allows to use each housing (villa and guest house) independently of each other.
